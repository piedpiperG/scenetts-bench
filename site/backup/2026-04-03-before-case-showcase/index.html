SceneTTS-Bench: A Benchmark for Scene-Level TTS in Drama Dubbing

SceneTTS-Bench is a scene-level benchmark for drama dubbing TTS, designed to evaluate whether systems can preserve character identity, express dramatic tension, and keep long-form rhythm coherent across multi-turn dialogue.

160 bilingual scenes ~10,300 utterances SCS / UAR / RDR

Overview

About This Work

SceneTTS-Bench reframes TTS evaluation around scene-level performance in drama dubbing. Instead of scoring isolated utterances only, it checks whether a system can act consistently across a whole scene.

The benchmark focuses on three dimensions that matter in multi-role dialogue: SCS for timbre consistency across character turns, UAR for under-acting on high-tension lines, and RDR for rhythm discontinuity under segmented long-form synthesis.

A backend-agnostic Canonical IR keeps evaluation conditions aligned across different TTS systems, while automatic pipelines produce interpretable scene-level diagnostics instead of a single undifferentiated score.
