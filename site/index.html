Scene-Level TTS Benchmark

Benchmarking drama dubbing beyond the sentence level.

SceneTTS-Bench evaluates text-to-speech systems on speaker consistency, emotional expressiveness, and rhythm coherence across multi-role dialogue scenes.

Quick Start Explore Metrics
SCS

Speaker Consistency Score

Measure timbre drift across the turns of the same character.

UAR

Under-Acting Ratio

Detect whether dramatic lines are performed with insufficient emotional intensity.

RDR

Rate Discontinuity Ratio

Capture rhythm jumps between segments split from long-form synthesis.

Why It Matters

Drama dubbing fails at scene boundaries, not just sentence quality.

Existing TTS evaluation protocols focus on isolated utterances. SceneTTS-Bench reframes evaluation around the failure modes that become visible only inside multi-role dialogue scenes.

Core Metrics

Rhythm Coherence
